Privacy Policy

Conductor Studio

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Conductor Studio is a desktop-first analysis tool. We do not sell personal information, run advertising trackers, or collect analytics telemetry about how you use the app.

Information handled locally

Conductor Studio is designed to process your device-analysis files on your own machine. Files you open in the app, generated results, templates, app settings, and local workspace preferences are stored locally on the device you control unless you explicitly export or upload them yourself.

Network access

The application may access network resources for:

  • checking for and downloading software updates
  • opening online documentation or support pages
  • AI-related or cloud-connected features explicitly initiated by the user

Outside of these cases, Conductor Studio is intended to work primarily with local files and local application data.

Information sharing

We do not share your files or personal information with third parties for advertising or profiling. If you choose to use a third-party or cloud-connected feature, only the information needed for that action is sent to the service you selected.

Updates and diagnostics

Desktop releases may contact GitHub-hosted update endpoints to check whether a newer version of the app is available. This update check is used only to deliver software updates.

Conductor Studio does not intentionally include advertising SDKs or usage analytics telemetry in its normal desktop workflow.

Policy changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
